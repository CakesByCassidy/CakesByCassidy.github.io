About

Welcome to Cakes By Cassidy

Hello! I'm Cassidy, and I'm so glad you're here. Food is my love language. Like many others, food is how I express myself, how I show affection, and how I experience the world. Nothing brings me more joy than knowing my creation made someone smile.

If you're here to purchase a baked good, check out our weekly specials. If you're an avid baker (like me) stop by the blog for home baking tips. While you're at it, grab a cupcake or two.
