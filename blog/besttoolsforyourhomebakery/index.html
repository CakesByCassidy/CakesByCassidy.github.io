Best Tools for Your Home Bakery

Published on: February 6, 2022 | 2 minute read
Tags: Beginner Tools

Hello again! I am back and I am here to help you start your own home bakery, and what better way to start than to obtain all the necessary tools for your bakery. These will be a good start to any bakery and any speciality you are catering toward. Here are 7 of my recommended kitchen tools.

  1. KitchenAid Hand Mixer Pretty self explanatory, but this will save you heaps of time and pain by mixing for you. Your cookies can be done in no time with this KitchenAid hand mixer.
  2. KitchenAid Stand Mixer The stand mixer will really reduce any work you have to do manually. So, if you had a tiresome arm workout yesterday then rest assured, you will have no work to do with this stand mixer AND you will still get to enjoy all the sweet treats that come with it.
  3. Rubber Spatula This is absolutely a necessity. Not only does the rubber make it easy to wash off, but it makes it very easy to lick clean too!
  4. Mixing Bowls Good mixing bowls are hard to come by, but with this set you will be sure to have every size you need. So, far those busy holiday baking needs, you can whip up 5 different treats at once.
  5. Silicone Baking Mat The silicone baking mat is a very nice tool to have when you are just starting out baking. It makes the clean up process a lot less painful.
  6. Wilton Piping Tips These are a great way to make any treat look beautiful yet professionally done. Feel free to purchase any piping tips that get you excited to bake. The Wilton ones are my favorite.
  7. Wilton Piping Bags Finally, I suggest piping bags. While they aren’t necessary, they will make the decorating process much easier and smoother.

I hope you found my tools useful and if you have any other suggestions for beginner home bakers, please let me know! I wish you the best of luck in all of your baking adventures.
